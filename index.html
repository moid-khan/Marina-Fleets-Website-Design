About Us

The trusted name in yachting and boating

Welcome to the world of Marina Fleets, here we all share one passion: the freedom that only luxury yachting and boating offers. And undoubtedly you share the same passion, whether you are looking for an unforgettable yachting and boating escape, considering making the step towards ownership or are an Owner wanting to manage and maximise your investment. Whatever your requirement, you need a partner to see your unique vision fulfilled dependably and effortlessly.

Yachts For Sale

Marina Fleets is the world leader in the sale of luxury yachts and boats over 30m. We offer an extensive range of new and used superyachts, Boats, Motorboats for sale and have access to thousands more, while our advanced databases allow us to bring buyers and sellers together easily and discreetly. However our true advantage lies in the specialist expertise of our team, our brokers view hundreds of vessels every year and build personal relationships with hundreds of Owners worldwide.
